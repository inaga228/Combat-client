# Changelog

## [1.0.0]
### Added
- Initial release of Combat Client for Forge 1.16.5
- **Combat** modules: KillAura (CPS/range/target mode), Reach, AntiKnockback, AutoCrystal, Velocity
- **Renderer** modules: ESP (box/outline, color picker), Trajectories, Chams, FullBright, NoWeather
- **HUD** modules: Info HUD (FPS/coords/biome/speed), ArmorHUD, PotionHUD, Notifications
- **Player** modules: NoFall, FastPlace, AutoEat, Sprint (Legit/Omni), AntiVoid
- ClickGUI — open with Right Shift
  - Left-click to toggle modules
  - Right-click to open settings panel
  - Drag panels freely
  - Slider settings adjustable by clicking on the bar
  - Keybind row: click "Bind" then press a key
